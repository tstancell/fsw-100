Incremental workloads are projects that are expected to add new Stakeholders or change the existing services and applications. The required data will be provided by the DEVSECOPS team to the Capacity Management team.

Service Capacity Management (SCM) addresses the current state of production-service levels versus specific service targets documented in established SLOs. The SCM answers the question: “Are established production-service agreements currently satisfied?” SLOs, expressed as response times are the target values.

Key Performance Parameters (KPP) are attributes or characteristics of a system that are considered critical or essential to the USCIS mission. SLOs are used to identify the nominal range or to identify whether the measured KPP value is within tolerance.
