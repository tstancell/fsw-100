Tamara's Global Event Venue Resources

No cost to all Combat Wounded Warriors and Service Disabled Veterans

Our site provides information on event venues worldwide. Through sponsorship and a large donation network, we are able to work with event planners worldwide and negotiate scheduling, pricing, catering, entertainment, site design, and transporation on behalf ofnmour clients. This process allows us to find event venues that are not being used which includes vacation homes, ballrooms, beach homes, private boats, castles, libraries, gyms, and practically any area you can think of that can be used to support client taste. We will never we provide discounts to the military, police, forces, firefighters, and first responders. There will never be a cost associated to combat Veterans or service disabled vets.

Due to the current COVID-19 Crisis, we are primariliy supporting virtual livestreaming and recorded events. We are in the process of ensuring all chosen venue site have proper COVID-19 mitigation and that we meet CDC thresholds. Thank you for your support and donation, we will continue to provide weekly updates HAVE A GREAT, BLESSED, AND WODERFUL SUMMER!!!!!!!!!!!!.
